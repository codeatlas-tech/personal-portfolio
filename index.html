About

Built through execution, not credentials

Learning systems by building, breaking, and deploying them in the real world.

How I work

I started building systems early—hardware, robotics, automation—long before formal education mattered. What stuck wasn’t theory, but how systems fail under real constraints.

After school, I chose execution over safe degrees. Some projects failed, others shipped. Every iteration sharpened one insight: most businesses collapse under operational complexity.

Efficidex is the result of that learning—turning chaotic, human-heavy workflows into structured, software-driven systems.

Principles

Reality over assumptions. Shipping over planning. Automation only matters if it survives real usage.

Current focus

Building Efficidex as an autonomous operations layer for real businesses, starting with hospitality and service-heavy industries.
